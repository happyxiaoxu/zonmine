Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Lootlister?

    Lootlister enables users that are using the eBay/Amazon arbitrage drops shipping business model to quickly find the best selling items in other eBay seller’s stores with the highest profits, and list them in your store.

  • What do I get when I sign up?

    You get instant access to the Lootlister webapp and companion Scout tool that helps you narrow down the products you want to list.

  • What is eBay/Amazon arbitrage

    This is a method of drop shipping products on eBay using Amazon.com as the drop shipping supplier. You create eBay listings of Amazon products and when someone buys an item from your eBay listing they pay money into your account. You take that money and purchase the item from Amazon, but instead of shipping it to yourself you input the eBay buyer’s shipping address. Amazon ships the product to the buyer and you collect the profit between the elevated price you list it for on eBay and the cost of the item on Amazon.

  • What is drop shipping?

    Drop shipping is when you the seller have a supplier and you sell their products which they inventory. When you make a sale you pay your supplier for the item using the buyer’s money and your supplier ships the product to your customer. The best thing about it is you don’t have to deal with the hassles of shipping and inventorying products.

  • What if I have never sold on eBay before?

    That’s okay. You can start using the eBay/Amazon arbitrage method immediately and generate sales. With Lootlister the best thing is all of your items in your store will have high selling potential.

  • Do you provide training for using Lootlister?

    We provide training for using the Lootlister webapp and companion tools in the members area.

  • Is Lootlister available in other countries than USA?

    Currently Lootlister is only available for USA eBay/Amazon, but we are looking for beta testers to help us develop for other countries. If you would like to help us please contact us!
